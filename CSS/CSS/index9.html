Community Bulletin

The city council has introduced several new programs for improving public engagement and community development. Residents are encouraged to participate in workshops, cultural programs, and awareness campaigns conducted throughout the year. New parks, road developments, and smart city projects are also under progress. Citizens can register online for volunteering activities and community support programs. The administration aims to create a cleaner, safer, and greener environment for all residents. Public meetings will be held every month to discuss upcoming improvements and gather suggestions from citizens. Everyone is welcome to participate and contribute ideas for the betterment of the community.
